# Meal Portion

Re-portion a logged meal. Scale all its ingredients proportionally and add them to your queue or share them with someone else.

## How to Get Here

On the **Log** screen, tap the **time and macro row** at the top of any meal card.

## What You See

### Meal Summary

The meal's timestamp and total weight in grams.

### Desired Total Grams

A text field pre-filled with the meal's current total weight. Change it to scale the whole meal up or down. As you type, every ingredient and the macro totals update in real time.

### Ingredient List

All items in the meal, each showing:

- Original grams
- Scaled grams (based on what you typed)
- Scaled calories and protein

### Scaled Macro Totals

A summary row showing the proportionally-scaled calories, protein, fat, carbs, and fiber for the new total weight.

## Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Add to Queue** | Adds every scaled ingredient to your Log Queue as individual items |
| **Share** | Opens the Portion Sharing QR screen so you can send the scaled ingredients to another device |
| **Cancel** | Returns to the Log screen |

!!! example "Example: Splitting dinner"
    You logged a dinner that totals 800g. Your partner wants to log their half. Tap the meal header, change the total to `400`, and tap **Share**. They scan the QR code and get 50%-scaled versions of every ingredient in their queue.
